## Icon and right section

You can add an icon to the left and right sides with the `icon` and `rightSection` props respectively.

<ThemeProvider>
  <TextInput
    placeholder="TextInput with icon and rightSection"
    icon={<SearchIcon />}
    rightSection={<StarIcon />}
  />
</ThemeProvider>
