## Overview

IconButton are clickable elements that are used to trigger actions. They
communicate calls to action to the user and allow users to interact with pages
in a variety of ways.

<ThemeProvider>
  <IconButton>
    <ChevronRightIcon />
  </IconButton>
</ThemeProvider>

## IconButton sizes

IconButton has four different sizes: `xs`, `sm`, `md` and `lg`.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group style={{ gap: 32, padding: 8 }}>
    <IconButton size="xs">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
    <IconButton>
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
    <IconButton size="md">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
    <IconButton size="lg">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
  </Group>
</ThemeProvider>

## IconButton variants

IconButton has `default` variant and `transparent`.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group style={{ gap: 32, padding: 8, backgroundColor: 'whitesmoke' }}>
    <IconButton size="lg">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
    <IconButton size="lg" variant="transparent">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
  </Group>
</ThemeProvider>

You can also change its radius to `rounded` or `default`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group style={{ gap: 32, padding: 8 }}>
    <IconButton size="lg" color="primary">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
    <IconButton size="lg" rounded color="primary">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
  </Group>
</ThemeProvider>

## IconButton colors

IconButton has three different colors: `positive`, `negative` and `primary`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group style={{ gap: 32, padding: 8 }}>
    <IconButton size="lg" color="positive">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
    <IconButton size="lg" color="negative">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
    <IconButton size="lg" color="primary">
      <ChevronRightIcon />
    </IconButton>
  </Group>
</ThemeProvider>
