## Overview

BooleanInput is a input for boolean values, it's a wrapper for `Checkbox` and `Switch` components.

You can choose which input to show with the `display` prop, which accepts: `checkbox` and `switch`.

<ThemeProvider>
  <BooleanInput label="Boolean input" />
  <BooleanInput display="switch" label="Boolean input" />
</ThemeProvider>

## BooleanInput sizes

When using `display="switch"`, BooleanInput accepts three different sizes: `xs`, `sm`, `md`:

<ThemeProvider>
  <BooleanInput display="switch" label="Switch label" size="xs" />
  <BooleanInput display="switch" label="Switch label" size="sm" />
  <BooleanInput display="switch" label="Switch label" size="md" />
</ThemeProvider>

## BooleanInput variants

The BooleanInput component has two variants: `default` and `boxed`. It also has a `disabled` state.

<ThemeProvider>
  <BooleanInput label="BooleanInput 1"></BooleanInput>
  <Space></Space>
  <BooleanInput label="BooleanInput 2" variant="boxed"></BooleanInput>
  <Space></Space>
  <BooleanInput label="BooleanInput 3" disabled></BooleanInput>
</ThemeProvider>

## Help and label positions

You can also place the help text to the right or bottom of the BooleanInput label.

<ThemeProvider>
  <BooleanInput label="BooleanInput 1" help="Right help"></BooleanInput>
  <Space></Space>
  <BooleanInput label="BooleanInput 2" help="Bottom help" helpPosition="bottom"></BooleanInput>
</ThemeProvider>

And you can place the label with the `labelPosition` prop to: `start` or `end`

<ThemeProvider>
  <BooleanInput label="BooleanInput 1"></BooleanInput>
  <Space></Space>
  <BooleanInput label="BooleanInput 2" labelPosition="start"></BooleanInput>
</ThemeProvider>
