## Overview

Switch is a component to capture boolean input from user

<ThemeProvider>
  <Switch label="Switch label" />
</ThemeProvider>

## Switch sizes

Switch accepts three different sizes: `xs`, `sm`, `md`:

<ThemeProvider>
  <Switch label="Switch label" size="xs" />
  <Switch label="Switch label" size="sm" />
  <Switch label="Switch label" size="md" />
</ThemeProvider>

## Switch variants

The Switch component has two variants: `default` and `boxed`. It also has a `disabled` state.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Switch label="Switch 1"></Switch>
  <Space></Space>
  <Switch label="Switch 2" variant="boxed"></Switch>
  <Space></Space>
  <Switch label="Switch 3" disabled></Switch>
</ThemeProvider>

## Help and label positions

You can also place the help text to the right or bottom of the switch label.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Switch label="Switch 1" help="Right help"></Switch>
  <Space></Space>
  <Switch label="Switch 2" help="Bottom help" helpPosition="bottom"></Switch>
</ThemeProvider>

And you can place the label with the `labelPosition` prop to: `start` or `end`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Switch label="Switch 1"></Switch>
  <Space></Space>
  <Switch label="Switch 2" labelPosition="start"></Switch>
</ThemeProvider>
