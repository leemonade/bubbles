## Overview

ScoresBar is a component that renders a graphic of the distribution of scores.

You can provide a `minimumGrade` and the scores greater than the minimum will show in green, the scores smaller will show in red and if its the same it will show in yellow

<ThemeProvider>
  <Box style={{ height: 200 }}>
    <ScoresBar scores={FULL_SCORES} grades={GRADES} />
  </Box>
</ThemeProvider>

## ScoresBar variants

ScoreBar has two variants: `multicolor` and `onecolor`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Box style={{ height: 200 }}>
    <ScoresBar scores={FULL_SCORES} grades={GRADES} />
  </Box>
  <Box style={{ height: 200, marginTop: 16 }}>
    <ScoresBar scores={FULL_SCORES} grades={GRADES} variant="onecolor" />
  </Box>
</ThemeProvider>

## ScoresBar marker

With the `withMarker` prop and `markerLegend` you can display a marker at the level of the `minimumGrade`.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Box style={{ height: 200 }}>
    <ScoresBar scores={FULL_SCORES} grades={GRADES} withMarker markerLegend="Minimum grade" />
  </Box>
</ThemeProvider>

## Left legend and bar percentage

You can choose to not display the leftLegends with `showLeftLegend` and to display the percentage of the score in the bar with `showBarPercentage`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Box style={{ height: 200 }}>
    <ScoresBar
      scores={FULL_SCORES}
      grades={GRADES}
      showBarPercentage={true}
      showLeftLegend={false}
    />
  </Box>
</ThemeProvider>
