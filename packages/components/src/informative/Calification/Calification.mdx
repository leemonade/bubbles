## Overview

Calification is a component to display a calification, you must provide a `grade` and a `minimumGrade`.
It will appear in green if the grade is larger than the minimumGrade, red if it's smaller or yellow if it's even.

You can also provided a `label` to show with the grade.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Stack direction="column" spacing={2} fullWidth>
    <Calification label="Passed :)" minimumGrade={5} grade={8} />
    <Calification label="Label" minimumGrade={5} grade={5} />
    <Calification label="Failed :(" minimumGrade={5} grade={3} />
  </Stack>
</ThemeProvider>

## Orientation

You can also change it's orientation, it can be `horizontal` or `vertical`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Stack direction="row" spacing={2} fullWidth>
    <Calification label="Failed" minimumGrade={5} grade={3} orientation="vertical" />
    <Calification label="Passed :)" minimumGrade={5} grade={8} styles={{ width: '50%' }} />
  </Stack>
</ThemeProvider>

## Inverted colors

With the `inverted` prop, you can invert the colors of the number and label sections.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Stack direction="column" spacing={2} fullWidth>
    <Calification label="Passed" minimumGrade={5} grade={8} />
    <Calification label="Passed" minimumGrade={5} grade={8} inverted />
  </Stack>
</ThemeProvider>
