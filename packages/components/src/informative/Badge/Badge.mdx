## Overview

Badge is a component to display some information about something.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Badge label="Badge" />
</ThemeProvider>

## Badge sizes

Badge has three different sizes, `xs`, `md` and `lg`. The large size can not have an avatar.

## Badge colors

Badge has two different colors, `solid` (grey) and `stroke` (white).

<ThemeProvider>
  <Group spacing="xl">
    <Badge>Small badge</Badge>
    <Badge size="md">Medium badge</Badge>
    <Badge size="lg">Large badge</Badge>
  </Group>
  <Box style={{ marginTop: 16 }} />
  <Group spacing="xl">
    <Badge color="stroke">Small badge</Badge>
    <Badge color="stroke" size="md">
      Medium badge
    </Badge>
    <Badge color="stroke" size="lg">
      Large badge
    </Badge>
  </Group>
</ThemeProvider>

## Badge closable

Setting the `closable` prop, which is `true` by default to `false`, you can hide the close button.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Badge closable={false}>No close button</Badge>
</ThemeProvider>
