## Overview

Modal is a component to display a modal. To explore all the props, go to the story.

It is controlled with the `opened` prop.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Modal>
    <Text>Im the content of a modal</Text>
  </Modal>
</ThemeProvider>

## Modal sizes

Modal has four different sizes: `xs`, `sm`, `md` and `large`

## Modal title

You can provide a `title` to Modal and center it with the `centerTitle` prop

## Close button

Also it accepts a `closeButton` prop that will display a close button.
