## Overview

Drawer is a component to display an area at any side of the screen. To explore all the props, go to the story.

<ThemeProvider>
  <BaseDrawer size={150} opened />
</ThemeProvider>

## Drawer positions

Drawer can appear from 4 different positions: `left`, `right`, `top` or `bottom`.

## Drawer sizes

There are six different sizes: `xs`, 'sm', `md`, 'lg', `xl` and `full`. Also you can pass a number in `px` or a percentage of the screen.

## Drawer overlay and shadow

You can open the Drawer with an overlay for the rest of the screen with `withOverlay` and control the opacity with `overlayOpacity`.

You can also choose to display a shadow or not with the `shadow` prop.

## Drawer back button

You can render a back button with the `back` prop.
