## Overview

Title is a component to render title headings.

<ThemeProvider>
  <Title>Title</Title>
</ThemeProvider>

## Title orders

Title has six different orders/sizes: `1`, `2`, `3`, `4`, `5` and `6`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Title order={1}>Title 1</Title>
  <Title order={2}>Title 2</Title>
  <Title order={3}>Title 3</Title>
  <Title order={4}>Title 4</Title>
  <Title order={5}>Title 5</Title>
  <Title order={6}>Title 6</Title>
</ThemeProvider>

## Title transform

Like with Text, you can transform the title text to: `capitalize`, `uppercase`, `lowercase` or `none`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Title transform="capitalize">Capitalized title</Title>
  <Title transform="uppercase">Uppercase title</Title>
  <Title transform="lowercase">Lowercase title</Title>
  <Title>Normal title</Title>
</ThemeProvider>

## Title colors

You also have five different colors to choose: `interactive`, `primary`, `secondary`, `tertiary` and `soft`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Title color="interactive">Interactive</Title>
  <Title color="primary">Primary</Title>
  <Title color="secondary">Secondary</Title>
  <Title color="tertiary">Tertiary</Title>
  <Title color="soft">Soft</Title>
</ThemeProvider>
