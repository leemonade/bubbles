## Overview

TextClamp is a component that truncates text at a specific number of lines. With the `lines` props you can choose how many line you want to appear.

        elementum, mattis lectus sit amet, pretium neque. Donec bibendum tempor neque, et malesuada

## TextClamp options

You can choose to not show a tooltip when hovering the text setting the `withTooltip` prop to false.

<ThemeProvider>
  <TextClamp withTooltip={false}>
    <Text>
      Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Phasellus ac eros sed mi finibus
      placerat id vitae neque. Vivamus volutpat lacus sed sodales hendrerit. Nullam et elit
      elementum, mattis lectus sit amet, pretium neque. Donec bibendum tempor neque, et malesuada
      ligula tincidunt nec.
    </Text>
  </TextClamp>
</ThemeProvider>

You can also show a showMore/showLess button with the `withToggle` prop. You will need to also provide the `showMore` and `showLess` label props.

<ThemeProvider>
  <TextClamp withToggle>
    <Text>
      Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Phasellus ac eros sed mi finibus
      placerat id vitae neque. Vivamus volutpat lacus sed sodales hendrerit. Nullam et elit
      elementum, mattis lectus sit amet, pretium neque. Donec bibendum tempor neque, et malesuada
      ligula tincidunt nec. Proin tincidunt eros non posuere semper. Quisque ac commodo massa.
      Aenean fringilla leo a aliquam ornare. Nam id est lorem. Ut quis urna orci. Pellentesque
      libero lorem, imperdiet eget purus a, vestibulum tristique nibh. Proin felis velit, interdum
