## Paragraph align and clean

You can align the text in Paragraph to `right`, `left` or `center`, and choose to delete all default margins with `clean`

<ThemeProvider>
  <Box style={{ display: 'flex', flexDirection: 'column', gap: 16 }}>
    <Paragraph>Paragraph aligned to left</Paragraph>
    <Paragraph align="center">Paragraph aligned to center</Paragraph>
    <Paragraph align="right">Paragraph aligned to right</Paragraph>
  </Box>
</ThemeProvider>

## Paragraph other props

Paragraph is a wrapper around Text, so it has all other props of Text.
