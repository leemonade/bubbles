## Overview

Alert is a component to display alerts with different types of severity

<ThemeProvider>
  <Alert title="Info" severity="info" variant="inline" action="Do something">
    Brochure is missing
  </Alert>
</ThemeProvider>

## Alert variants

We have two alert varians: **Inline (by default)**, and Block

<ThemeProvider>
  <Alert
    title="Info"
    severity="info"
    variant="inline"
    action="Do something"
    style={{ marginBottom: '1rem' }}
  >
    Brochure is missing
  </Alert>
  <Alert title="Info" severity="info" variant="block" action="Do something">
    Amet minim mollit non deserunt ullamco est sit aliqua dolor do amet sint. Velit officia
    consequat duis enim velit mollit. Exercitation veniam consequat sunt nostrud amet.
  </Alert>
</ThemeProvider>
