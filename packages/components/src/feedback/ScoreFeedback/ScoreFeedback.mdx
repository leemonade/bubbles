## Overview

ScoreFeedback is a component that displays a `Calification`, with a custom feedback node.

<ThemeProvider>
  <ScoreFeedback calification={{ minimumGrade: 5, grade: 5.2, label: 'FB', showOnlyLabel: false }}>
    <Box style={{ padding: 32 }}>
      <Text color="primary">This is the feedback for your calification...</Text>
    </Box>
  </ScoreFeedback>
</ThemeProvider>
