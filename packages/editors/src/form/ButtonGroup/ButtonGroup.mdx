## Overview

ButtonGroup is a component to group `Button` elements and give them styles depending on position. It will accept `Buttons` and any `Tool` element.

<ThemeProvider>
  <ButtonGroup>
    <Button label="Button 1" icon={<StarIcon />} />
    <Button label="Button 2" icon={<LadybugIcon />} />
    <Button label="Button 3" icon={<LikeIcon />} />
  </ButtonGroup>
</ThemeProvider>
